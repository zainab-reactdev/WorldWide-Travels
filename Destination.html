TURKEY

Natural beauty of Turkey ranges from fairy chimneys in Cappadocia to turquoise coastlines in Antalya. You can also opt for balloon rides across Cappadocia's peculiar landscapes or hike through its picturesque valleys.

MALDIVES

The Maldives officially the Republic of Maldives, and historically known as the Maldive Islands, is a country and archipelagic state in South Asia in the Indian Ocean.

ENGLAND

England is a country that is part of the United Kingdom.[7] It is located on the island of Great Britain, of which it covers about 62%, and more than 100 smaller adjacent islands.

SINGAPORE

Singapore is a sunny, tropical island in Southeast Asia, off the southern tip of the Malay Peninsula. It is inhabited by five million people from four major communities; Chinese, Malay, Indian and Eurasian.

FRANCE

France, in Western Europe, encompasses medieval cities, alpine villages and Mediterranean beaches. Paris, its capital, is famed for its fashion houses, classical art museums including the Louvre and monuments like the Eiffel Tower.

GREECE

Greece is a country in southeastern Europe with thousands of islands throughout the Aegean and Ionian seas. Influential in ancient times, it's often called the cradle of Western civilization.

CANADA

Canada is a country in North America. Its ten provinces and three territories extend from the Atlantic Ocean to the Pacific Ocean and northward into the Arctic Ocean, making it the world's second-largest country by total area.

INDONESIA

Indonesia, officially the Republic of Indonesia, is a country in Southeast Asia and Oceania, between the Indian and Pacific oceans. It consists of over 17,000 islands, including Sumatra, Java, Sulawesie.

BRAZIL

Brazil, officially the Federative Republic of Brazil, is the largest and easternmost country in South America and Latin America. It is the world's fifth-largest country by area and one of the most populated countries.
